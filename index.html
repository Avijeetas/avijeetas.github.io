Research

I am a PhD researcher at the Text and Web Mining Lab, University of North Texas, advised by Dr. Wei Jin. My work focuses on semantic query interpretation for Question Answering systems, with a strong emphasis on interpretability and explainability in retrieval pipelines. Recent work accepted at RANLP 2025 and WI-IAT 2025.

My research spans Text & Web Data Mining, Information Retrieval, and Biomedical & Healthcare Informatics, with recent publications at RANLP 2025 and WI-IAT 2025. I hold 100+ citations on Google Scholar with an H-index of 2.
